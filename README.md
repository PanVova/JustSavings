# JustSavings
In this interesting application you can set your future goals. You can create a new a goal . Set a name for it and amount of money you have to collect you can even set a photo to your desire. It will be randomned 1 in 17 . Also you can set a currency sign to your goals . Such as $ or â‚¬. Inside of the goal you can set an amount to deposit or withdraw . By the way , you can set to what date you want to fulfill your goal . It will be shown to you have much you have to deposit everyday. Also , this application has 2 modes : White and Dark it can be changed by the phones settings (Google whats the Dark mode is ).
